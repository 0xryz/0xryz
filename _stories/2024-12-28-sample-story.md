## The Beginning

My journey into bug bounty hunting started when I discovered my first vulnerability. It was a simple XSS, but it opened my eyes to the world of security research.

## The Discovery

After hours of testing, I found a reflected XSS vulnerability that could be exploited to steal user sessions. The excitement of that moment is something I'll never forget.

## Lessons Learned

1. **Persistence pays off** - Don't give up when initial tests fail
2. **Think outside the box** - Sometimes the vulnerability is where you least expect it
3. **Document everything** - Good documentation makes all the difference

## Moving Forward

This experience motivated me to continue learning and improving my skills in web application security.
